Domain Generalization (PACS)

This is the track of the state-of-the-art methods on PACS benchmark.

Domain generalization (DG) assumes a model is trained from multiple observed domains while it is expected to perform well on any unseen domains. PACS consists of Art painting, Cartoon, Photo and Sketch domains, which so far considers the largest domain shift from the different image style depictions.
